What is boba?

Boba is made from tapioca starch, also known as cassava starch, which is why they're often also labeled as "tapioca pearls."

Why to drink boba shakes?

Adding boba to your smoothie makes it unique and super fun, not to mention all the health benefits that comes with drinking smoothies on a regular basis.
